Shipment Optimization Analysis For Yappi Cookies Industries

OBJECTIVE

To optimize the shipment process for Yappi Cookies Industry by identifying factors contributing to shipping delays and their impact on revenue. This will involve analyzing shipment data to uncover key insights and trends, and developing an analysis that provides actionable recommendations to improve shipment efficiency, reduce delays, and increase revenue

METHODOLOGY

Our methodology followed these steps
  • Bottom Up & Top Down Exploration
  • Identify important features
  • Defined HSA
  • Recommendation

ANALYSIS

We identified the following results
  • Shipment duration has effect on revenue.
  • Happiness processes goods within an average of two days.
