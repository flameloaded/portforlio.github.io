Diagnostic Analysis on Cookies Business Using Power BI

Executive Summary: Diagnostic Analysis of Low Purchase from Promo and Acme Grocery Store

Introduction: OLK Store, a wholesale cookies retailer, has been experiencing low purchases from two significant customers, Promo and Acme Grocery Store. This diagnostic analysis aims to uncover the root cause of the low purchase and present key performance indicators (KPIs) such as total transactions, total profit, total revenue, total number of cookies sold, and production cost. The analysis was conducted as part of an intern project, involving data cleaning and modeling using Power Query and Power BI. Data Preparation: The raw data, spanning four years and organized into separate Excel sheets, was imported into Power Query. The data underwent cleaning to address null values and unwanted figures. Afterward, it was loaded into Power BI for further analysis.

Data Modeling: A critical step in the process involved the creation of a one-to-many relationship between the fact table and dimension table. This relationship was pivotal for connecting transactional data with relevant dimensions for a comprehensive analysis.

    Key Findings: The dashboard revealed several key insights for the current Year:
  • Total transactions: 707
  • Total profit: $62.8 million
  • Total revenue: $627.8 million
  • Total number of cookies sold: 371,000
  • Production cost: $588 million

Profit Analysis by Year: 2019: A downward trend was observed from the first quarter to the third quarter, followed by an upward trend in the fourth quarter.

2020: An upward trend was noted from the first quarter to the second quarter, followed by a steep downward trend from the third quarter to the fourth quarter.

Total Transactions by Customer: A cluster bar chart depicted that Acme Grocery Stores and Promo had 49 and 14 transactions, respectively, categorizing them as low-purchasing customers.

Total Transactions by Shipping Duration: An analysis of shipping duration revealed that all transactions with Promo and Acme Grocery Store experienced delays. This delay may be a contributing factor to the low purchase volumes from these customers.

Recommendation: Based on the insights gathered from the dashboard, it is deduced that the low purchase from Promo and Acme Grocery Store could be attributed to delayed shipments. Therefore, it is recommended that OLK Store addresses and improves the efficiency of its shipping processes to enhance customer satisfaction and potentially increase purchases from these clients.

Conclusion: This diagnostic analysis provides a comprehensive overview of OLK Store's transactional data, identifying specific issues with two key customers. By addressing the highlighted shipping delays, OLK Store can potentially enhance its relationship with Promo and Acme Grocery Store, leading to increased sales and improved overall performance.
