Sales Analysis on OLK Cookies Industries LTD.

Executive Summary: Descriptive Analysis on OLK STORE

Introduction: OLK Cookies Industries LTD, a company that produces cookies for human consumption, commissioned a comprehensive analysis to elucidate critical aspects of its business operations. The focus of this analysis was to delineate key performance indicators (KPIs) that encompassed total transactions, Gross profit, total revenue, gross profit margin, and production costs. The project additionally delved into customer segmentation based on the quantity of cookies purchased, with a keen examination of the associated shipping durations. Executed as part of an intern project, this analysis undertook meticulous data cleaning and modeling procedures employing advanced tools such as Power Query and Power BI. The utilization of these sophisticated analytical tools ensured a rigorous and insightful examination of OLK Industries operational dynamics, allowing for a nuanced understanding of various facets crucial to strategic decision-making and business optimization. The outcomes of this project contribute valuable insights for refining business strategies and enhancing overall performance within the sales of cookies.

Data Preparation: The raw data, spanning four years and organized into separate Excel sheets, was imported into Power Query using append and merge. The data underwent cleaning to address null values and unwanted figures. Afterward, it was loaded into Power BI for further analysis.

Key Findings: The dashboard revealed several primary key insights for overall year:

  • Total transactions: 3231
  • Total gross profit: $279 million
  • Total revenue: $3 billion
  • Gross profit margin 10.00%
  • Production cost: $3.78 million

Customer Segment: Establishments demonstrating transactional volumes equal to or exceeding 300 were categorized as high-purchasing customers, reflecting a substantial level of engagement and business activity. Conversely, establishments with transactional volumes below 300 were classified as low-purchasing customers, indicating a comparatively moderate level of business interaction. This classification framework provides a structured approach to assessing the performance and purchasing behavior of each customer, facilitating a nuanced understanding of their respective contributions to the overall business landscape.

Shipments Duration Segment: The categorization of shipment durations is established based on a discerning criterion: durations equal to or less than 3 are classified as expedited or fast shipments, whereas durations exceeding 3 are designated as standard or slower shipments. This meticulous classification system enables a nuanced evaluation of shipping efficiency, allowing for a more strategic and informed approach to logistics management.

A critical step in the process involved the creation of a one-to-many relationship between the fact table and dimension table. This relationship was pivotal for connecting transactional data with relevant dimensions for a comprehensive analysis.

Customer Lifetime Value

Cascade Grocers (CG) emerges as the leading entity, boasting a figure of $517 million in 2020, followed closely by Quick Bite Convenience Store (QBC) with a respectable value of $430 million. This data underscores CG's prominence as the most valued customer based on Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) analysis, as depicted in the chart.

Customer Lifetime Value in respect to duration of shipment

Based on the scatter plot analysis, it is evident that Cascade Grocers exhibits a higher Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) when shipment duration is lower, and conversely, a lower CLV when shipment duration is higher. This observation suggests a clear relationship between shipment duration and business performance. Specifically, it indicates that longer shipment durations may have a detrimental impact on the business's overall profitability, while shorter durations correlate with higher CLV.

Recommendation:

Optimization of Shipping Processes:

Given the impact of shipment duration on customer satisfaction and business performance, it is recommended to conduct a comprehensive review of shipping processes. This may involve streamlining operations to reduce shipping durations, particularly for expedited shipments. Improved logistics efficiency can contribute to enhanced customer experience and loyalty.

Strategic Customer Engagement:

The customer segmentation analysis identified establishments with transactional volumes equal to or exceeding 300 as high-purchasing customers. It is advisable to implement targeted engagement strategies for these customers, such as personalized offers, loyalty programs, or exclusive promotions. Strengthening relationships with high-purchasing customers can have a positive impact on overall business performance.

Continuous Monitoring and Adaptation:

The dynamic nature of performance trends, as evident from the yearly analyses, underscores the importance of continuous monitoring and adaptability. OLK Store should establish a proactive approach to monitor market changes, customer behaviors, and internal operations, allowing for timely adjustments to business strategies.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, the sales analysis conducted on OLK Industries LTD. using Power BI has provided valuable insights into various facets of the business, ranging from customer transactions to revenue and production costs. The detailed examination of key performance indicators and trends over the years has offered a comprehensive understanding of the business dynamics. The findings emphasize the need for strategic adaptations in shipping processes and customer engagement strategies. Continuous monitoring and proactive decision-making are crucial for navigating the evolving market landscape. By implementing the recommended strategies and maintaining a flexible approach, OLK Cookies Industries can position itself for sustained growth, improved customer relations, and optimized operational efficiency. This analysis serves as a foundation for informed decision-making and strategic planning to drive the future success of OLK Cookies Industries.
